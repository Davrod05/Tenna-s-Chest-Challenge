HOW TO PLAY:
- Click the chests to find hearts, their locations are randomized every time.
- You get to open 3 chests at the time.
- You get points depending on how many chests it took you to find a heart:
  1 chest: 30 points
  2 chests: 20 points
  3 chests: 10 points
- If you open 3 chests and found no heart you get 10 points removed from your score.
- Get 100 points in 30 seconds to win.
